Kyle Mesdag - Designer / Stand up Comedian / Digital Media Creator

I've been thinking with computers, websites and graphics for as long as I can remember. When I was younger I used to make my own holiday and birthday cards for my family. I used to love making them! So it felt like an easy transition into graphic design and digital media. I have worked with clients like the Lowertown Brewery, Calabogie Brewing, myMarketing.io, The Marketing Station, The Aggressive Good and many more. Be sure to check out my portfolio for a list of my work. When I'm not make something look better you can catch me on stage around the country telling jokes or whitewater rafting on the Ottawa River. If you think we would work well together, please don't hesitate to reach out.
