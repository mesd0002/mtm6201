PORTFOLIO ABOUT CONTACT

I have been tinkering with graphics since I was a young kid. As a kid, I took a lot of pleasure in being creative and making holiday and greetings cards for my family and friends. A wise man might tell you that it is “the thought that counts”, but I think I was just being cheap. Though, it really paid off!

I graduated from Algonquin College in 2013 from a 3-year advertising and marketing communications program with an advanced diploma. I was really able to hone in on my design and copywriting skills while working for the school magazine “Glue” and during a six-week internship at Zespa Media in Beijing, China.

I thoroughly enjoy freelancing as I am able to fully embrace my creativity and exercise a broad range of skills that I am passionate about. The flexible schedule also provides me the freedom to pursue additional courses and education at Algonquin College, as well as my other two passions; stand-up comedy and whitewater rafting.
